Works

These have been my main projects for the last six years at Yle - Finnish Broadcast Company. Actually these aren’t just projects, these are services that we continuously develope. Designs are made in collaboration with design and development teams. Currently, I work with Yle Areena web and big screen applications.    

Yle Areena web application

Yle Areena is video and audio service that shows all Yle's TV and radio programs online. I'm doing UI / UX design and some visual design in Yle Areena.

Yle Areena video and audio player

Media players are the heart of Yle Areena service. Players are embeddable and used in other Yle web services. I contributed to UI and visual design.

Yle.fi frontpage

Yle.fi is the home of all Yle's web services. It was complitely redesigned during the last quarter of the year 2015. I was designing user interface as a part of UX design team.
