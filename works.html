Works

These have been my main projects for the last six years at Yle - Finnish Broadcast Company. Actually these aren’t just projects, these are services that we continuously develop. Designs are made in collaboration with design and development teams. Currently I work with Yle Areena web application.    

Yle Areena web application

Yle Areena is Yle's video and audio streaming service. I design mainly service's UI / UX but I do also some visual design if necessary.

Lasten Areena web application

Lasten Areena is Yle's video service for children. Lasten Areena includes content for children only. User interface and interactions are designed simple and and easy to use for the little ones.

Yle.fi frontpage

Yle.fi is the home of all Yle's web services. It was complitely redesigned during the last quarter of the year 2015. I was designing user interface as a part of UX design team.

Yle Areena video and audio player

Media players are the heart of Yle Areena service. Players are embeddable and used in other Yle web services. I contributed to UI and visual design.
